Our Identity

Who We Are

APSI Technologies — also known as Auxwit Technologies Private Limited — is an agricultural robotics and intelligence company headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Founded with a singular conviction that Indian agriculture deserves the same quality of intelligent automation that advanced manufacturing has enjoyed for decades, we exist to close that gap — not with imports or half-measures, but with technology built from the ground up for Indian soils, Indian crops, and Indian farmers.

Our flagship product, i-Farmate, is the culmination of that mission: a fully autonomous field robot that navigates, detects, intervenes, and reports without human intervention. But i-Farmate is only the most visible expression of a broader philosophy — that every layer of the agricultural value chain, from soil intelligence and drone mapping to direct-to-consumer commerce and certified organic produce, can be improved through thoughtful, farmer-first technology.

We are engineers, agronomists, data scientists, and farmers' children. We have walked fields at 4am. We have watched crops fail to pests that could have been caught a week earlier. We have seen the arithmetic of farming — rising labour costs, falling margins, climate unpredictability — squeeze the life from families who have farmed the same land for generations. That is the problem we are solving, and we take it personally.

Our incubation at NAARM's a-IDEA program in Hyderabad, our partnership with Vellore Institute of Technology, and our presence at India's leading agri-tech expos have validated that what we are building is both technically sound and commercially necessary. We do not chase technology for its own sake. Every feature in i-Farmate, every data point in TerraMate, every listing on FarmFair, is there because a farmer asked for it or because our field research showed it would make a measurable difference.

We build with empathy, ship with rigour, and measure success in yield improvements and lives made easier. At APSI Technologies, we believe the future of food is intelligent, chemical-free, and farmer-owned. We are building it, one field at a time — and we invite you to join us on that journey toward a more sustainable, more equitable, and more productive agricultural future for India and the world.
